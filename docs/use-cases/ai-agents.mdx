Build AI agents that can handle multi-step processes, recover from failures, and coordinate complex workflows involving multiple AI models and external systems.

Perfect for AI agents that need to:
- Execute long-running research tasks that survive interruptions
- Coordinate multiple AI model calls with error handling
- Handle timeouts and retries when calling AI APIs
- Maintain workflow progress across system restarts
- Process large datasets reliably without losing progress

## Why resilience matters for AI

AI agents often involve unreliable external calls (API timeouts, rate limits, model failures) and long-running processes. Restate ensures your AI workflows complete successfully even when individual components fail.
